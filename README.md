I'm a student who builds across the whole map — AI workflows, autonomous robots, games, mobile apps — usually by picking something I don't know how to make yet and figuring it out in public. I'm headed toward **frontier machine learning**, **product engineering**, or **forward-deployed engineering**: work that's part exploration, part meeting people where their problems actually live.

*What I've spent real hours on, by terrain:*
